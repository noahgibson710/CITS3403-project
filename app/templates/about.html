About

Our calculator provides precise estimates of your daily macronutrient requirements (protein, carbohydrates, and fats) to help you reach your fitness goals. Whether you're trying to lose weight, build muscle, or maintain a healthy lifestyle, understanding your macros is key.

How It Works

By entering your age, weight, height, activity level, and fitness goals, our calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to determine your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). From there, it calculates the optimal macronutrient distribution for your specific needs.
